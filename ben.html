Artificial Intelligence in Education

AI has the potential to improve the education sector such as automating admin tasks or providing specialised curriculm to students

Here are some various uses of Artificial Intelligence Technology in

Education

Personalised Education

focus on different areas for each student. improve academic performance and speed up process

Automated Grading / Admin Tasks

Teachers spend most time planning and completing admin work. AI can perform mundane tasks faster than teachers. Teachers have more time to teach content and help students

Virtual Learning Environments

Online learning which can be accessed anywhere in the world. Allowed learning outside physical classroom
