Artificial Intelligence in Education

AI has the ability to create a brighter future

Here are some various uses of Artificial Intelligence Technology in

Education

Personalised Education

AI can use data around student attention, interest, aptitude, # and ability to recommend Personalised coursework. This will help build the capability of students towards a specific career path which is suited to their strengths and interests.

Automating Tasks

Currently, AI can automate grading for nearly all kinds of multiple-choice and fill-in-the-blank exams. As essay-grading software continues to evolve, this range of capabilities will continue to expand. Automating mundane tasks will allow teachers to spend more time in front of students and will overall improve the quality of education.

Virtual Learning Environments

E-learning from an AI tutor involves that learners are free to gain deep understanding. Because students have more time to reflect or relax if they need. Artificial Intelligence trainers are more knowledgeable and less error-prone. Online learning allows students to get an education anywhere in the world including outside physical classrooms
